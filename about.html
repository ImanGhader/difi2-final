Me, Me, Me!

I am a passionate photographer. As I grew up, I fell more and more in love with photography. It has made me look at life in a whole new way, and has definitely changed my perspective on a lot of things. I love how, through photography, you can keep memories alive, and cherish them forever.

I enjoy traveling to many different countries, as I spend my time in different places, I get to see many new faces and different cultures. This inspires me a lot, especially when I am holding my camera. I like to think of it as seeing the world through my camera’s lens.
